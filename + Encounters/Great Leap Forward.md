The [[Great Leap Forward]] was an industrialization campaign within China from 1958 to 1962, led by the Chinese Communist Party during [[Chairman Mao]] authority
**Result**
- Caused 15 and 55 million deaths in mainland china during 1959-1961, In just three years.
 > Kinda crazy how one guy and his crazy policies could cause an entire country to go to shit.
- Largest or second largest famine in the human history
- The [[Great Leap Forward]] was overwhelmingly a failure: it did not succeed as intended, and its legacy is mainly one of human suffering and economic collapse
