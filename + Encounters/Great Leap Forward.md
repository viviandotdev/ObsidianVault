The Great Leap Forward was an industrialization campaign within China from 1958 to 1962, led by the Chinese Communist Party.
**Result**
- Caused 15 and 55 million deaths in mainland china during 1959-1961, 
- Largest or second largest famine in the human histroy


**what was the goal of the great leap forward?**
- Collectivizing agriculture 
	- Instead of working on their own plots for individual profit (capitalist), farmers would work on larger farms, sharing resources, equipment and often profits. However the majority of the profits went to the state.
	- **Consequences**
		- Peasants lost the incentive to work hard since the profits were. mostly taken by the state
		- Lack of motivation and mismanagement caused the collective farms to be less productive.
