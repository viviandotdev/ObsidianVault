In general people see anger as an impulsive, instantaneous emotion, however Adler argues that anger is manufactured to achieve a goal.

For example a mother can be on the phone and kindly request something from someone and they instantly yell at her daughter right after.
She clearly has control over he anger and when she wants to express it.

I don't believe in anger because anger creates vertical relationships, in order to express anger you have to make someone else feel less than
