A good argument in five sentences will sway more people than a brilliant argument in a hundred
sentences. Don’t fight it.

Learn how brains organize ideas

- Keep things simple
- Prune your sentences to get rid of extra words
- Grab the reader in the first sentence
- Write short sentences. Avoid putting multiple thoughts in one sentence. Readers aren’t as smart as you’d think.
- Place the person before the action.
