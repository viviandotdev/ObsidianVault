## Slow Carb Diet

### Rules
1. Don't drink calories
2. Don't eat fruit
3. Eat at least 20 grams of protein per meal
4. Eat 30 grams of protein within 30 minutes of waking up
5. Drink 64 oz of water
6. 10k steps

### Meals
1. Breakfast
	-  2 scrambled whole eggs with a protein shake
2. Lunch
	- Salmon
3. Snack
	- Greek Yogurt
4. Dinner
	- Chicken
