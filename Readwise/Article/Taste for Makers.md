- But if your job is to design things, and there is no such thing as beauty, then there is *no way to get better at your job.* If taste is just personal preference, then everyone's is already perfect: you like whatever you like, and that's it.
  As in any job, as you continue to design things, you'll get better at it. Your tastes will change. And, like anyone who gets better at their job, you'll know you're getting better. If so, your old tastes were not merely different, but worse. Poof goes the axiom that taste can't be wrong. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kf8hh02fcbs0ft18dkg406fk))
