# Consistency Building Framework

This framework offers a progressive, three-level approach to building daily consistency, discipline, and effective study habits.
## How It Works
The core idea is to gradually increase your daily commitment, ensuring 100% consistency at each stage before advancing.
### Levels

*   **Level 1: Foundation**
    *   **Duration:** 15 days
    *   **Daily Commitment:** 15 minutes
    *   **Breakdown:** 13 minutes Input, 2 minutes Output
    *   **Goal:** Achieved with **100% consistency** daily.

*   **Level 2: Expansion**
    *   **Duration:** 30 days
    *   **Daily Commitment:** 30 minutes
    *   **Breakdown:** 25 minutes Input, 5 minutes Output

*   **Level 3: Mastery**
    *   **Duration:** 30 days
    *   **Daily Commitment:** 1 hour
    *   **Breakdown:** 45 minutes Input, 15 minutes Output

### Key Principles

*   **Discipline & Habits:** Develops self-discipline, awareness, and patience through a structured, increasing routine.
*   **Gradual Progression:** Time commitments increase slowly to build strong habits without burnout.

### Tracking Progress

*   **Visualize Your Wins:** Use pen and paper to manually track and mark off each consistent day. Seeing your progress helps maintain motivation and commitment.

### Expected Outcomes

Upon completion, learners will have cultivated robust routines and habits necessary for sustained learning, preparing them for more complex challenges in their chosen field.
