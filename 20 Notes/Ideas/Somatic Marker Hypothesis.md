The Somatic Marker Hypothesis - our decisions are not purely logical, "somatic markers" such as gut feelings or physiological signals help quickly bias our choice towards advantageous outcomes.
Similar to the System 1 (Fast thinking), responsible for our immediate reactions.


**Releated**
[[Thinking Fast and Slow]] by Daniel Kahneman
